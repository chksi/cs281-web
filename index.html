Objective

The goal of the project is to explore that could be submitted to a top-tier machine learning conference such as NIPS, ICML or AISTATS.

Collaboration

You should work in a group of 3 or 4 people. Groups of 2 will be considered with permission. Larger groups will not be permitted.
